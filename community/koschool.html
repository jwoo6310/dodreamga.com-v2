About Our School

Established: September 2009

Affiliation: National Association of Korean Schools (NAKS) and Southeast Korean School Association

We provide Korean language and cultural arts education for children in bilingual living environments, helping them develop creative and flexible thinking through collaboration between teachers, students, parents, and associations.

We offer various Korean cultural experiences and events such as traditional market days to introduce Korea to our students.

Our Mission

Language Education

Comprehensive Korean language instruction tailored for bilingual children

Cultural Immersion

Authentic Korean cultural experiences and traditions

Community Building

Fostering connections between students, families, and educators
