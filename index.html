Welcome

A Message from Pastor David Kim

Dear friends in Christ, it is with great joy that I welcome you to DoDream Korean American Baptist Church. Our name "DoDream" comes from the Korean word meaning "to pray" and reflects our commitment to being a house of prayer for all nations. Here in Buford, we strive to create a spiritual home where Korean-Americans and all who seek God can grow in faith, serve in love, and worship in spirit and truth. Whether you're visiting for the first time or looking for a church family, we invite you to join us on this journey of faith.

Rev. David Kim
Senior Pastor

Our Ministries

There's a place for everyone to serve

Worship Team

Our worship team leads the congregation in vibrant praise and worship each Sunday. Whether you sing or play an instrument, we'd love for you to join us in magnifying the Lord.

Youth Ministry

Engaging middle school through college-age students with Bible study, fellowship, and fun activities that help them grow in their relationship with Christ.

Women's Ministry

A sisterhood of women supporting each other through Bible studies, prayer meetings, and special events designed to encourage spiritual growth.

Men's Fellowship

Monthly gatherings where men connect over food, fellowship and God's Word, encouraging each other to live out biblical manhood.

Discipleship Training

Classes for all ages where we study God's Word together. From toddlers to seniors, there's a class to help you grow spiritually.

Missions

Supporting missionaries locally and globally, with annual mission trips that allow our members to be the hands and feet of Jesus.
